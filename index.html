About Me

I was born in a quaint and pretty place called Hasimara located near the northern tip of West Bengal, India. My schooling, from kindergarten to senior secondary, was from the Holy Cross School Agartala, India.

I moved to Bhubaneswar for my college where I did my 5-year integrated MSc in Physical Sciences at the National Institute of Science Education and Research (NISER).

During and after my integrated master's degree, I worked on research projects at various institutes before joining the Thermofluidics and Nanotechnology for Sustainable Energy Systems Laboratory, IIT Kharagpur, for my doctoral studies.

Personal Interests: As an avid football enthusiast, I love playing football. Whenever I miss the field too much, I watch the sport. I am also passionate about travel, especially on my motorcycle, and hope to find many more less-explored places.
